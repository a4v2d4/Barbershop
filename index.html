Our Story

A full-service barbershop at the heart of downtown Cotati — where Sonoma County families have trusted us with their hair for over 30 years.

Right in the Heart of Downtown

Find us on the back side of La Plaza Park — conveniently located in the hub of Cotati.

Licensed Barbers & Stylists

Every member of our staff is a licensed barber or stylist with decades of experience — specializing in all types of clipper cuts.
